A Brief on Job Scams

  • Reported losses: Increased more than threefold from 2020 to 2023, with over $220 million reported in just the first half of 2024.
  • Task scams: Reports of these scams quadrupled from 5,000 in 2023 to 20,000 in the first half of 2024.
  • Cryptocurrency losses: Job scams involving cryptocurrency hit $41 million in the first half of 2024, nearly double the amount reported in all of 2023.
  • Common methods: Scams often start with unexpected texts or WhatsApp messages offering online work, asking victims to complete simple tasks like liking videos or rating products.
  • Victim demographics: A significant number of victims are young adults who are often lured by the promise of easy money.
